WITHDRAWN: Typhoon Bopha: Child Protection in Emergencies

To ensure the identification and registration of unaccompanied and separated children (UASC), facilitate family reunification, and provide follow up care; To establish Child-Friendly Spaces (CFS) or Safe Spaces (including women) in communities for safe play, psychosocial support and referral services; To develop and strengthen Community-Based Protection Networks in affected communities to prevent and respond to child abuse, exploitation, violence and trafficking; To maintain adequate data and information management and coordination mechanisms with and between agencies with respect to child protection; To build the capacity of government in meeting its responsibilities and accountabiliity towards children and women, in preventing and responding to issues of abuse, exploitation and violence .
