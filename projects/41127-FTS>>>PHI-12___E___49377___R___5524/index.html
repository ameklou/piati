(WITHDRAWN) Tropical Storm Washi: ECCD in Emergencies: Training on Indigenous Toy Production and Basic Psychosocial Processing for Day Care Workers and Child Development Workers

1.To provide early childhood educational materials that can be used by day care workers/ preschools teachers/volunteer facilitators in holding learning sessions while in evacuation areas. 2.To create opportunities for children to play as a way of helping them cope with psychological stress brought about by the disaster. 3.Identify locally available materials that can be used as tools for learning. 4.Identify indigenous games that can be included in their session plans. 5. Share basic information on psychosocial processing
