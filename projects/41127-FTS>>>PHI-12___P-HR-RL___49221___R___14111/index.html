Tropical Storm Washi: Children and Youth Development through Psychosocial Support Services, Ensuring their Welfare and Protection

- Provide Children and Youth Development Center for Psychosocial Support , well-being, guidance and recreational spaces for the Washi affected children and youth to bring normalcy and facilitate healing process; - Ensure the protection measures at the community level for children and youth affected by Storm Washi, so that they will be free from any child-abuse, sexual gender based violation (SGBV), exploitation, and trafficking; - Equip children, youth and families with appropriate awareness on Child Rights, Child Protection in Emergencies, SGBV, CY Welfare and Development, and Disaster Risk Reduction Management (DRRM) principles. - Inculcate into the importance of savings and investment towards the development of livelihood and enterprise for home based growers/producers to support children's needs.
