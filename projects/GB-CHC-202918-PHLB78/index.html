Mindanao Community of Practice on Women's Economic Leadership

This project aims to integrate the WEL framework in the work of selected OMP partners. To achieve this aim, the project will conduct a participatory research to determine the means by which such an integration can happen and to develop a community of practice (CoP) among OMP partners to monitor the impacts of WEL integration, evaluate the outcomes against pre -set goals, and a learning platform to contnue improving on WEL practice.
