PH Mindanao Rural Dev. Project - Phase 2

The development objective of the Second Phase of the Mindanao Rural Development Project for Philippines is to: improve livelihood opportunities of targeted communities; and institutionalize a decentralized system for agriculture and fisheries service delivery that promotes participation, transparency and accountability. The changes in the proposed restructuring involve the provision of a US$3.0 million grant financing from the Australia-World Bank Philippines development trust fund to support capacity and institution building activities included in the project's component on Investments for Governance Reform (IGR) and program administration. The main activities to be funded under the grant were originally appraised when the main loan project was appraised on August 29, 2006.
