Economic Justice Campaigns and Alliances

Implementation

Through this project, Oxfam will be campaigning on food justice through its GROW campaign, by building alliances and movements, and working with partners on issues around agriculture and food, climate change and land rights. All advocacy and campaign initiatives will keep women's rights and issues at the center of the campaign.
