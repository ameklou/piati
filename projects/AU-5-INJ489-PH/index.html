Contracts Management Levy 2010-11

The Contracts Management Levy directly supports procurement and contract management processes for delivery of aid programs. It also funds legal services (clearances) for all tenders, contracts, grants and other forms of agreement with partner governments and multilateral organisations. The total value of this multi-country initiative is $5.3 million over 3 years, starting 2009-10.
