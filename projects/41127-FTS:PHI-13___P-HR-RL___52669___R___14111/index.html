Ensuring protection of children through community mobilization and networking mechanism

•To strengthen child protection mechanism in support to government services through advocacy promotion on the rights of the child for boys, girls and CWDs in all ages in returned and resettlement sites of IDPs and IPs; •To re-organize/revive community-based child protection network (CBCPN) that sustains child protection mechanism and disaster risk reduction management (DRRM) for girls, boys and CWD during emergencies with the support of government agencies, stakeholders and child protection advocates; •To capacitate network committees to assess, plan, facilitate trainings, help child registration, monitor, document, do referrals, and report on the violations of child rights. •To establish Child-Friendly Spaces (CFS) that nurture neglected children, protect and develop the mental, emotional, psychological, social and physical well-being of both boys, girls and CWDs with needed provisions considering gender issues.
