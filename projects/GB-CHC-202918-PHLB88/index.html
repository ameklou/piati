Strengthening Governance in Conflict-affected and Fragile States in Mindanao

Implementation

This is governance advocacy project aimed at developing a reform agenda that will lead to improved governance, better delivery of social services and an enabling political environment with which peace and development could proceed within the areas covered by the ARMM. The project will contribute to the broader CSO efforts in the region on promoting responsive, accountable and transparent governance through sustain conduct of citizen's satisfaction and monitoring system, monitoring LGU compliance on full disclosure policy, budget and project analysis, and advocacy for institutionalizing participation and mechanisms in the regional level.
