What is this site?

This site shows data that donors have already published about their aid activities in the Philippines – both existing activities and in response to Typhoon Haiyan.

Where does the data come from?

Donors have only just begun to publish to IATI (the International Aid Transparency Initiative), so the data will currently be far from complete, and the quality depends on the individual donors.

The site is updated each night, but donors generally publish much less frequently, so it may be some time until it is complete.

Why publishing data is important

Publishing more data about aid activities should help everyone gain a better understanding of the overall picture. In a humanitarian situation, there are much greater and more urgent needs, so coordination is vital.

What can I do?

In the short term, donors could help by providing their data to UNOCHA's Financial Tracking System (FTS). In the medium term, publishing to IATI will help to automate and speed up the process.
